About FinSmart.

Born from the DASHHACK 1.0 initiative, FinSmart addresses the critical gap in financial literacy among young adults.

Our mission is simple: To turn financial anxiety into financial clarity through intuitive, data-driven tools. We believe that when you can visualize your money, you can master it.
